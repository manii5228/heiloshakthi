Solar Panel Maintenance Guidelines

  • Check for dirt, debris, bird droppings, or physical damage like cracks and scratches.
  • Ensure no objects (trees, buildings) cast shadows on the panels.

  • Use clean water and a soft cloth or sponge to remove dirt and grime.
  • Avoid using abrasive tools or high-pressure water. Clean early morning or evening to prevent streaks.

  • Compare current energy production with expected levels.
  • Investigate sudden drops in performance via a monitoring system.

  • Inspect cables and connectors for wear or damage.

  • Perform voltage and current tests to ensure optimal system performance.
